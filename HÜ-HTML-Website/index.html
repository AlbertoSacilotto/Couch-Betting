What are the contents of the website?

The Website is premier league only and it contains:

 • A "Betting" section where you can bet without money (website-currency-betting)

 • A "Fantasy League" section where you can have your own 15 players bought with the website-currency

 • A "News" section where you can see some of the most interesting news in the premier league

Betting

    The betting is 100% free you only need to log in to get your website-points.

    You are able to bet on premier league games.

    You can bet on:

     • on score

     • on the winner

     • on a draw

     • on score before the 2nd half

     • on goal diffrence

    If you dont have enough money to bet you will only be able to bet "on score", which is harder.

Fantasy League

    The "Fantasy League" is 100% free you only need to log in to get your website-points

    You have some starting points to buy 15 players (with strategy)

    If your player performs well you will get points for:

     • each goal a striker/middlefield player shoots

     • each save by a keeper (you get more points for a penalty save)

     • each clean sheet by the goalkeeper/defender
